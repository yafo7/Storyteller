# Default Product Constitution

Apply these defaults when the user delegates per-game decisions.

- Product: offline-first 2D narrative adventure with staged performance and bounded exploration.
- Scope: 30-60 minutes, 3-8 maps, one complete ending path, optional interpretive variation.
- Adaptation: preserve immutable facts and reveal order; allow mechanic translation, spatial consolidation, and connective dialogue.
- Player: choose the role that creates the strongest dramatic limitation and most useful verbs without granting omniscience.
- Design: derive one core mechanic from a repeated action, rule, object, spatial contradiction, or central metaphor in the source.
- Interaction: every required interaction changes at least two of knowledge, possession, position, access, relationship, danger, actor intent, audiovisual state, or world layer.
- Art: create story-specific art with a coherent art bible; use external assets only when their license and provenance are explicit.
- Runtime: deterministic core progression, no client secrets, no required live AI, reversible saves.
- Accessibility: remappable controls, readable CJK text, captions, reduced motion, high-contrast focus, and no clue conveyed through one sensory channel only.
- Autonomy: generate and score alternatives internally. Do not ask the user to choose individual mechanics, lines, layouts, or images.

Escalate only rights questions, canon-critical ambiguity, prohibited content, unavailable required tools, or a requested scope beyond the supported product class.
