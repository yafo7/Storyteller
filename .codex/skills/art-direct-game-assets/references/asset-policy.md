# Asset Source Policy

## Source priority

1. User-owned or project-owned assets with known rights.
2. AI-original assets created from the approved art bible and identity anchors.
3. CC0 or public-domain generic assets.
4. Compatible attributed or purchased assets with recorded terms.
5. Geometric placeholders during development only.

Never copy a visual merely because it is publicly visible. Record the exact source URL or local provenance and license evidence.

## License gates

- `CC0`: reusable without conditions; still record provenance.
- `CC BY`: record creator and required attribution.
- `CC BY-SA`: use only when the project's distribution terms are compatible.
- `CC BY-NC`: reject for commercial-capable production unless the charter explicitly restricts use and accepts the risk.
- `CC BY-ND`: reject when cropping, recoloring, animation, compositing, or other adaptation is required.
- Unknown or custom terms: block until verified.

## AI generation hierarchy

Generate in this order: style frame, character identity sheets, environment keyframes, base maps, map-state variants, portraits, sprites, props, UI, polish variants. Reuse approved references at every dependent step.
